New York Comic Extraordinaire

 

Akash Anand is a comedian, writer, producer and animator currently living in NYC. He was passed at the Comic Strip at 19 and performed on New York Comedy Festival at 20.
 
Akash's stand-up comedy centers around daily observations he makes about the world as well as his own life experiences.

 

 
 
 
 
 

Biography

 

Originally born in Chennai, Akash is a comic who has his roots in Singapore, India, Chicago, and New Jersey. He speaks three languages, and has a family who is very supportive of his comedy aspirations. Before starting in comedy, Akash was a failed entrepreneur.
