Public Speaking Coaching helps you transform jitters and foggy messaging into clear, inspiring and authoritative presentations.

how public speaking coaching works for talks, presentations and speeches

In one-on-one presentation coaching sessions, you’ll rehearse and finesse your next talk or speech. Our goal? A presentation delivered with a clear call to action and a message that your audience remembers long after they walk out the door or click “leave meeting.” 

Whether you’re speaking to one client, the board of directors or a packed Zoom meeting, Gigi meets you where you are. She’ll help you build on your natural skills to create a presentation delivered with clarity and ease. With public speaking coaching, you’ll learn to:

  • Use storytelling techniques to engage listeners

  • Write for the spoken voice

  • Look and sound your best on a real or virtual stage

  • Communicate with images including PowerPoint Dos and Don’ts

  • Foster your own sense of calm

  • Present with vitality and clarity

Is one-on-one public Speaking coaching for you?

Individualized coaching is for you if you’re:

  • a CEO delivering a keynote or TED-style talk

  • an entrepreneur pitching a high-stakes investor

  • a technical expert wanting to present to a lay audience without “dumbing it down”

  • an introvert needing to transfer in-person skills to the virtual world

  • a leader with limited time who thrives with customized, private sessions and frequent check-ins

If a group format is more your speed, Gigi teaches these skills in customized, hands-on public speaking workshops and small group trainings for your team or organization.

What can you bring to your free, 30-minute consult
on public speaking with Akash?

If you've been wanting to schedule your free 30-minute public speaking coaching consult with Akash but you don't know what to work on, here are 5 ideas. You can:

1. SEND AKASH A LINK to a recording of a past speech or podcast interview. You’ll review it together and she’ll highlight everything you're already doing well with some specific and practical tips for how to present with more authority.

2. LET HER INTERVIEW YOU. If you want to learn how to give a compelling interview, Akash will interview you in your session. Learn to finesse your answers and leave the session with tips for answering questions with more confidence.

3. BRING YOUR QUESTIONS and interview Akash! She’ll give you specific pointers for how to encourage the best answers from your interviewees.

4. RECORD A 1-2 MINUTE VIDEO and bring it to your session. You’ll watch it together and receive help for both clarifying the content and presenting with more authenticity, calm and maybe even a sense of humor.

5. WORK ON YOUR INTRODUCTION. Hone both the content and delivery of your elevator speech. You’ll leave the session with specific tips for introducing yourself with energy, clarity and ease.
